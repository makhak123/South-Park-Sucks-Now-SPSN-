## Deflationary Mechanisms

### Automatic Burns
Every transaction includes an automatic burn component, permanently reducing the circulating supply.

**Burn Rate Calculation:**
- Buy transactions: 1% of transaction amount
- Sell transactions: 2% of transaction amount

**Example:**
- If 1,000,000 SPSN are traded daily
- Average burn rate: 1.5% (mix of buys/sells)
- Daily burn: 15,000 SPSN
- Annual burn: ~5,475,000 SPSN (0.5475% of total supply)

### Manual Burns
The team can execute manual burns during special events:
- Community milestones
- Partnership announcements
- Quarterly burn events

## Anti-Whale Protection

### Maximum Transaction Limit
- **Max Transaction:** 1% of total supply (10,000,000 SPSN)
- **Purpose:** Prevents large dumps and promotes fair distribution
- **Exemptions:** Owner wallet during initial distribution only

### Blacklist Functionality
- Ability to blacklist malicious actors
- Protection against bots and scammers
- Can be removed if address is cleared

## Price Stability Mechanisms

### Liquidity Pool
- 40% of supply allocated to initial liquidity
- Continuous liquidity additions from transaction fees
- Liquidity locked for 12 months minimum

### Fee Structure
- Higher sell fees discourage panic selling
- Liquidity fees ensure deep order books
- Marketing fees support long-term growth
